Browser-Based 3D Aim Trainer (No Download)

Cyber.Aim is the ultimate online aim trainer designed for competitive players of Valorant, CS2, Overwatch 2, and Apex Legends. Unlike aimlabs or kovaaks, this runs directly in your browser with zero latency and 100% offline capability.

Technical Superiority

  • High Refresh Rate: Uncapped FPS support (144Hz, 240Hz, 360Hz).
  • Input Latency: Raw mouse input for 1:1 feel with your game.
  • Customizability: Adjust FOV, Sensitivity, and Crosshair to match your main game.

Pro Training Routines

  • Warmup: 5 mins of Gridshot (GRID.LOCK) to get blood flowing.
  • Precision: 10 mins of Spidershot (WIDOW_MAKER) focusing on under-flicking.
  • Smoothness: 5 mins of Tracking (ORBITAL_TRACK) to improve micro-adjustments.

Why Practice Aim Online?

Downloading heavy software just to warm up is obsolete. With WebGL 2.0 technology, Cyber.Aim delivers native performance without the bloat. Perfect for playing on school computers, library PCs, or just getting a quick warmup before a ranked match.
