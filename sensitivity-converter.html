Mouse Sensitivity Converter for FPS Games

Struggling to maintain your aim when switching between Valorant, CS2, Apex Legends, or Fortnite? Different game engines handle mouse input differently, meaning "Sensitivity 1.0" in one game feels completely different in another.

The DGToolbox Sensitivity Converter solves this by using precise 360° distance matching logic. We calculate exactly how many centimeters your mouse needs to move to turn a full circle in your current game, and then generate the mathematically perfect sensitivity value for your target game.

How to convert your sensitivity?

  1. Select your Source Game (e.g., Valorant).
  2. Enter your current Sensitivity and DPI.
  3. Select the Target Game you want to play (e.g., CS2).
  4. Enter the DPI you will use for the new game (usually keep it the same).
  5. Copy the Converted Value and paste it into your game settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this converter accurate for Valorant to CS2? The conversion is mathematically exact based on the yaw values of the Unreal Engine (Valorant) and Source 2 engine (CS2). Valorant sensitivity / 3.1818 = CS2 Sensitivity.
Q: Does changing DPI affect the conversion? Yes! If you change your mouse DPI, your effective sensitivity (eDPI) changes. Our tool allows you to input different DPI values for source and target to compensate automatically.
