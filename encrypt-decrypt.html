AES-256 Encryption is performed entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device.

Secure Online Encryption Tool

Crypto Studio uses the industry-standard Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) with a 256-bit key size to secure your text. This is the same encryption standard used by governments and security experts worldwide.

Because this tool runs 100% client-side (in your browser), your secret keys and data are never sent to a server. This ensures maximum privacy and security for your sensitive messages, passwords, or notes.

Popular Use Cases:
  • Encrypting sensitive emails before sending.
  • Storing secure notes in a public place.
  • Sharing secret messages via chat apps.
  • Generating and testing encryption keys.
