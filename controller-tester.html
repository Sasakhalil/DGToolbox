Complete Gamepad Diagnostics

The TITAN MK-II is a professional-grade gamepad tester designed to diagnose every aspect of your controller's performance. Whether you are competitive gamer needing to check for stick drift or a casual player ensuring your vibration motors work, this tool provides real-time telemetry.

Drift Analyzer

Analyze your analog sticks for centering errors. Our circular visualization highlights deviation from the perfect (0,0) center, helping you decide if you need to adjust your deadzones.

Polling Rate Test

Measure the input latency and update frequency (Hz) of your device. A higher polling rate (e.g., 1000Hz) means lower input lag for competitive shooters.

Haptics Engine

Test both high-frequency and low-frequency vibration motors. Customizable pulse duration helps verify if your controller's rumble features are fully functional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is my controller not detected?

Press any button on your controller to wake it up. Browsers require a user interaction (button press) before they allow websites to access the Gamepad API.

Q: What is a good polling rate?

Standard console controllers usually poll at 125Hz or 250Hz. High-end PC controllers and overclocked gamepads can reach 1000Hz (1ms delay) for maximum responsiveness.
