Why DGToolbox?

The web is full of "free tools" that are actually data traps. They upload your files, track your behavior, and bombard you with ads. DGToolbox was built to provide a sovereign alternative.

Who is this for?

Developers needing quick format conversions. Gamers optimizing assets for performance. Privacy advocates who refuse to upload personal data to the cloud.

The Philosophy

"If it can run in the browser, it stays in the browser." We leverage modern WebAssembly and JavaScript APIs to bring desktop-class performance to the web.

Core Principles

1

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We believe your data is none of our business. The app is architected so that we literally cannot see your files even if we wanted to.

2

Open & Transparent

No hidden scripts. No background miners. You can inspect the source code right in your browser dev tools and see exactly what's running.

3

Bloat-Free Experience

No newsletters popup. No "sign up to download". Just pure, unadulterated utility functions that do one thing and do it well.
